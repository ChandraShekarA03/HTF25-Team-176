The Midnight Mission

SpookyMart was founded on the simple, slightly eerie principle: **Every day is Halloween**. Our mission is to be the world's premier year-round source for the highest quality ghastly goods, from haunting home decor to wickedly wonderful wares.

We curate items that not only celebrate the macabre but also bring a touch of playful horror to your life, making the extraordinary ordinary.

From Crypt to Commerce

We started in a dusty, dimly-lit attic in Salem in 2020 by a trio of supernatural enthusiasts. What began as a small collection of hand-stitched ghosts and antique oddities quickly grew into the marketplace for the dark at heart.

Though we've moved to a slightly less dusty, more modern crypt, our commitment to artisanal, high-quality, and genuinely spooky products remains unshakeable.
